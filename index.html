ZEUS'S WRATH

Kraken's Grip

28/275 Nitrous

28/275 nitrous brawl.

  • Nitrous only
  • Limited to 699 CI or smaller
  • Limited to 28/275 or smaller slick or radial
  • No lock up
  • No billet blocks
  • No wheelie bars
  • Maintain factory body panels (aftermarket hood ok)
  • Maintain factory glass (Lexan windshield ok)
  • Maintain factory floor pans and firewall
  • Must have a dash (aftermarket ok)

Olympians Rumble

All Steel / Glass

All steel / all glass showdown.

  • Limited to 28/275 or smaller slick or radial
  • No lock up
  • No billet blocks
  • No wheelie bars
  • Maintain factory body panels (aftermarket hood ok)
  • Maintain factory glass (Lexan windshield ok)
  • Maintain factory floor pans and firewall
  • Must have a dash (aftermarket ok)
