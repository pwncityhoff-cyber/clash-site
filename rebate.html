$25 Racer Rebate Program

Purchase from supporting sponsors — get rewarded for it.

Back to Registration Questions? Contact Us

Purchase $25 or more from any Supporting Clash Sponsor and receive a $25 Clash Credit with valid proof of purchase.

Program Rules

  • Credits are issued 30 days after the completed race event
  • Purchases must be made within 30 days before or 30 days after the event
  • One receipt per class, per event
  • No duplicate receipts
  • Receipts must be clear and readable

Why This Matters

This program proves real return on investment for the companies that support Nitro Dave’s Clash of the Titans.

When you submit a receipt, that purchase is reported directly to the sponsor. They see exactly how many racers supported them because they supported the Clash.

Support those who support you — and get rewarded for it.
