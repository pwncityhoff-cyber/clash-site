Class Format: How It Works

  • All racers must be pre-registered prior to the event deadline.
  • Drivers Meeting: Chip Draw determines the ET Cap
  • Two qualifying passes determine the 32-car field.
  • The closest 32 racers to the ET Cap advance to eliminations (entries under the cap take priority).
  • Testing opportunities may be available prior to race day.

If the event promoter or track hosts a private test session, Clash of the Titans will share all relevant details so racers can participate.

If no private test is scheduled, Clash staff will work with the promoter and track to explore arranging one for participating racers.
